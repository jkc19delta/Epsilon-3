Epsilon 3 - A Stunning New Evolution

The Epsilon 3 represents the evolution of a stunning concept car to a dynamic demonstration of intent. This advanced new hydrogen-powered prototype opens the road for an exhilarating new chapter in sports cars and motorsport.

Aerodynamic Masterpiece

From front to rear, its carbon bodywork is carved for the racetrack. Aerodynamic new alloy wheels and magenta accents mark out Alpine’s ‘feel at one’ cockpit.

Hydrogen-Powered Performance

At its heart is a 4-cylinder internal combustion engine sending 340hp to the rear wheels via a 7-speed transmission. Competition-proven design ensures Epsilon 3 can support high cylinder pressures for hydrogen combustion.

Decarbonized Power

Decarbonized hydrogen and combustion engine technology pair up beautifully in Epsilon 3. Its bodywork contours around pressurized storage for over 6 kilos of hydrogen, previewing soaring but sustainable performance for both road and track.
